ACT CUBE. The Analogist® is a helping professional who relies on deep knowledge of Benemeglian Analogical Disciplines®, stemming from the discoveries of Stefano Benemeglio®, namely the laws and rules that govern emotional languages and human systemic dynamics. Through the study of Inductive Systems, Analogical Non-Verbal Communication®, Analogical Philosophy® and Fisioanalogy®, the Analogist® transfers tools and techniques that promote a revolutionary and healthy lifestyle aimed at profound and lasting wellbeing. The Analogist® also shares analogical knowledge through courses and training initiatives, conferences and seminars.

Method

The precise decoding of non-verbal language, through which a person’s emotional instance communicates every day before our eyes deep needs, rejections, preferences, disturbances and emotional blocks, is the method through which the Analogist® can lead people to know themselves as never before, bypassing conditioning and logical barriers, and identifying weaknesses and strengths capable of unlocking repetitive situations and relational or communication difficulties.

About

Antonio Tiziano Sergi, Analogist®, certified professional in Benemeglian Analogical Disciplines® and regularly enrolled in the Professional Register of Analogists - UPDA, Lecturer at U.P.D.A “Stefano Benemeglio” Popular University of Analogical Disciplines.

Deep knowledge of Benemeglian Analogical Disciplines® is also a valuable tool in professional, corporate and group contexts, to which the Analogist® can transfer innovative competences for reading emotional and relational dynamics and enabling truly effective communication.

Education

Comprehensive training in analogical disciplines is a three-year path designed to shape the Analogist® profile at the only accredited training institution, UPDA. By promoting the use of this new language, it fosters a new lifestyle inspired by Benemeglian Analogical Disciplines®, a model through which we can know ourselves deeply, learn to listen, and above all learn to govern the system — the meeting between our rationality and emotions — to move beyond the frequent passivity in which logic or uncontrolled impulsivity govern us instead of the opposite.

My Mission

I believe happiness and inner freedom begin with deep self-knowledge. My goal is to help you recognize and harmonize your emotions, so you can live more consciously, authentically and peacefully.

I carry forward Analogical Philosophy as a tool for growth, balance and personal fulfillment.
